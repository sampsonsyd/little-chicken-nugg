UNITY & SEPARATION

h

The concept of this space is derived from the work of Solange. It focuses on the choragraphy the dancers performed in her song “ Cranes in the Sky” and the art exhibit piece, Metatronia. As the dancers completed their sequence, the repetition and movements displayed unity and separation. Through their actions, I created a space that would act on those movements
