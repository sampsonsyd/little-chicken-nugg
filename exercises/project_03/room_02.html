Unity & Separation is a six-story performance hall that contains a theater, cafe, and multiple dance studios. The concept of this space is derived from the work of Solange. It focuses on the choragraphy the dancers performed in her song “ Cranes in the Sky” and the art exhibit piece, Metronia. As the dancers completed their sequence, the repetition and movements displayed unity and separation. Through their actions, I created a space that would act on those movements.
